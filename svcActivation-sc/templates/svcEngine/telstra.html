Internet Protocol Virtual Private Networks IP VPN are mainly used by big business, including government and enterprise. The basic concept of a VPN is to connect networks in separate offices and make them appear as one, or to connect remote individuals to their corporate network. This enables all employees connected to the VPN to interact with each other as though they are in the same building.

Business IP

For larger organisations that have more complex needs and a requirement for extra flexibility, Business IP may well be the answer. It is a sophisticated data network that allows you to work more efficiently while also identifying opportunities to reduce your communication costs. As an integrated Wide Area Networking WAN solution it offers a price per site model billed monthly, with minimal upfront costs, and allows you to use your compatible, existing customer premise equipment CPE.

Connect IP

Connect IP allows you to securely connect multiple business sites with reliability and ease. It is an ideal solution for small to medium size businesses, plus smaller branch sites of enterprise and government organisations. It is a fully integrated Wide Area Network WAN solution based on a price per site model that is billed monthly, with minimal upfront costs. Depending on which features you choose, the solution may include

  • On site equipment, including on site maintenance
  • Network access 64 kbps 100 Mbps
  • IP virtual private network VPN
  • Proactive on site equipment and network management
  • 2 Mbps secure Internet Gateway
  • Network design, configuration and management
  • Connect IP Telephony option for converged voice and data networking
